# Engineering Manager

El Engineering Manager es responsable de las **personas** y la **entrega** del equipo. Combina people management con execution management.

## ¿Qué es un Engineering Manager?

Un EM managea personas, no código. Su trabajo es crear el entorno donde los ingenieros puedan hacer su mejor trabajo, alineados con los objetivos del negocio.

> "An engineering manager's job is to multiply the output of the team." — Will Larson

## Responsabilidades Core

### People Management

#### 1:1s (Weekly)
| Elemento | Detalle |
|---|---|
| **Frecuencia** | Semanal, 30-60 minutos |
| **Propósito** | Career growth, feedback, impedimentos |
| **Estructura** | 70% persona / 30% proyecto |
| **Output** | Action items, follow-ups, notes |
| **Regla** | El direct report define la agenda |

**Template de 1:1:**
```
1. Check-in personal (5 min)
2. Prioridades de la semana (10 min)
3. Bloqueadores y soporte (10 min)
4. Career growth / feedback (10 min)
5. Acciones y next steps (5 min)
```

#### Career Growth
- Definir y mantener career ladders para el equipo
- Crear Individual Development Plans (IDPs)
- Identificar promotion candidates y preparar cases
- Conectar personas con stretch assignments

#### Hiring
- Definir job descriptions con [[../02-Estructura/06-product-owner|PO]] y Tech Lead
- Diseñar proceso de entrevistas (technical + cultural)
- Participar en entrevistas de culture fit
- Cerrar ofertas y manage expectations
- Diversity & inclusion en el proceso

#### Team Health
- Monitorear eNPS (Employee Net Promoter Score)
- Detectar y resolver conflictos temprano
- Prevenir burnout con workload management
- Fomentar psychological safety
- Celebrar logros y recognition

### Execution Management
- Coordinar delivery con PO y Tech Lead
- Remove impedimentos del equipo
- Stakeholder communication
- Sprint/iteration health monitoring
- Risk identification y escalation

## EM vs Tech Lead

| Dimensión | Engineering Manager | Tech Lead |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary focus** | People & delivery | Technical quality |
| **Who manages** | People (direct reports) | Code & architecture |
| **1:1s** | Yes, weekly | No (mentoring informal) |
| **Hiring** | Full ownership | Participa en technical |
| **Performance reviews** | Owns process | Provides input |
| **Code review** | Rarely | Daily |
| **Career conversations** | Primary owner | Supports |
| **Conflict resolution** | Primary owner | Escalates |

> En equipos pequeños, el EM puede actuar como Tech Lead. Pero a medida que el equipo crece, estos roles deben separarse.

## Modelo de 3 Horizontes

```
H1: Operaciones (60%)
├── 1:1s
├── Sprint health
├── Stakeholder management
└── Incident response (people side)

H2: Construcción (30%)
├── Team culture
├── Process improvement
├── Hiring pipeline
└── Cross-team coordination

H3: Estrategia (10%)
├── Org design
├── Technical strategy (input)
├── Budget planning
└── Long-term team vision
```

## Métricas del EM

| Métrica | Target | Cadencia |
|---|---|---|
| eNPS | > 30 | Quarterly |
| Retention rate | > 90% | Quarterly |
| Time to fill roles | < 45 días | Per hire |
| Team velocity stability | < 20% variance | Sprint |
| Engagement (1:1 completion) | 100% | Weekly |
| Career plan coverage | 100% del team | Quarterly |

## Team Size Óptimo

| Tamaño del team | Rol del EM |
|---|---|
| 3-5 personas | EM hands-on, possibly IC track |
| 6-9 personas | EM full-time, management focus |
| 10-14 personas | EM full-time, posibly needs support |
| 15+ personas | Split team, add EM or TL |

> Amazon y Spotify recomiendan teams de 6-8 personas como óptimo. Más de 10 empieza a perder cohesión.

## Anti-patrones del EM

| Anti-patrón | Problema | Solución |
|---|---|---|
| **The hero EM** | Hace todo, no delega | Trust the team, develop leaders |
| **The shield EM** | Aísla al equipo de toda info | Filtra, pero no bloque |
| **The micromanager** | Controla cada decisión | Define standards, da autonomía |
| **The absent EM** | Solo manages calendar | Dedicate time a people |
| **The promoter** | Solo dice sí al management | Empower the team to push back |
| **The tech-first EM** | Sigue coding, ignora people | Recognize the role shift |

## Frameworks Útiles

| Framework | Uso |
|---|---|
| **Radical Candor** | Feedback: care personally + challenge directly |
| **Situational Leadership** | Adaptar leadership style al contexto |
| **Top Grading** | Estructurar entrevistas de hiring |
| **OKRs** | Alineación de objetivos |
| **Career Ladders** | Framework de crecimiento |
