## Mentoring vs Coaching

| Aspecto | Mentoring | Coaching |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Enfoque** | Career growth, wisdom transfer | Performance improvement, skill building |
| **Relación** | Senior → Junior | Peer o External |
| **Duración** | Long-term (6-18 months) | Short-medium term (3-6 months) |
| **Agenda** | Mentor-led (guidance) | Coachee-led (discovery) |
| **Métrica** | Career progression, retention | Behavior change, skill improvement |

## Tipos de Mentoring Programs

### 1:1 Mentoring (Traditional)

| Aspecto | Best Practice |
|---------|--------------|
| **Matching** | Shared interests, career goals, personality fit |
| **Cadence** | 1 hora/quincenal, minimum |
| **Duration** | 6-12 months con option to renew |
| **Structure** | Mentee drives agenda, mentor provides perspective |
| **Documentation** | Shared notes, goals tracking |

### Pair Mentoring

- Dos personas de nivel similar aprendiendo juntas
- Mutually beneficial: different strengths
- Less hierarchical, more collaborative
- Good for cross-functional learning

### Reverse Mentoring

| Beneficiario | Quién enseña | Tema típico |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| Senior leader | Junior developer | New tech, tools, market trends |
| Engineering manager | IC senior | Hands-on skills, technical depth |
| Established team | New hire | Fresh perspectives, outside experience |

**Impacto**: Breaks hierarchy, builds empathy, accelerates learning bidirectionally.

### Group Mentoring / Mentoring Circles

- 1 mentor con 3-5 mentees
- Peer learning + mentor guidance
- Scalable when 1:1 isn't feasible
- Topic-focused cohorts

## Program Design Template

### Phase 1: Setup (Month 1)

| Activity | Owner | Timeline |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Define goals & success metrics | HR + Eng Leadership | Week 1 |
| Create mentee application form | Program manager | Week 2 |
| Recruit mentors (voluntary) | Engineering managers | Week 2-3 |
| Matching process | Program manager | Week 3-4 |
| Kickoff event | All participants | Week 4 |

### Phase 2: Active Mentoring (Months 2-8)

| Activity | Cadence |
|----------|---------|
| 1:1 meetings | Quincenal |
| Group check-ins | Mensual |
| Progress review | Trimestral |
| Mid-point survey | Month 4 |
| Mentor training | Once + refreshers |

### Phase 3: Wrap-up (Month 9)

| Activity | Propósito |
|----------|-----------|
| Final reflection session | Closure, celebration |
| Outcome survey | Measure impact |
| Success stories | Internal marketing |
| Program iteration | Improve for next cohort |

## Conversation Frameworks

### GROW Model (for Coaching)

| Letter | Question | Purpose |
|--------|----------|---------|
| **G**oal | "¿Qué quieres lograr?" | Clarity on objective |
| **R**eality | "¿Dónde estás ahora?" | Current state assessment |
| **O**ptions | "¿Qué podrías hacer?" | Generate alternatives |
| **W**ill | "¿Qué vas a hacer?" | Commitment to action |

### Mentoring Conversation Starter

1. "¿Cuál es tu mayor challenge ahora mismo?"
2. "¿Qué skill quieres desarrollar este quarter?"
3. "¿Cómo te ves en 2-3 años?"
4. "¿Qué obstáculos ves para llegar ahí?"
5. "¿En qué te puedo ayudar específicamente?"

## Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Patrón | Consecuencia |
|-------------|-------------|
| Forced pairing (no chemistry) | Waste of time, resentment |
| Mentor without training | Bad advice, missed opportunities |
| No time allocation | "I don't have time" kills program |
| Only for underperformers | Stigma, low adoption |
| No structure or goals | Drift, no measurable impact |
